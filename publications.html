Boundary Layer Theory

Auto-encoder-assisted analysis of amplitude and wavelength modulation of near-wall turbulence by outer large-scale structures in channel flow at friction Reynolds number of 5200

L. Agostini & M.A. Leschziner

Physics of Fluids, Vol. 34

2022

Show Abstract
This article presents a new methodology using an auto-encoder algorithm to separate large- and small-scale motions from extremely large direct numerical simulation fields of a channel flow at friction Reynolds number Reτ ≈ 5200. This approach overcomes the limitations of the empirical mode decomposition method previously used at lower Reynolds numbers.
